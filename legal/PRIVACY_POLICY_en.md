# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 2026**

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to GreenGo ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information
- Name and display name
- Date of birth
- Gender and gender preferences
- Email address
- Phone number (optional)
- Profile photos and videos
- Location data (with your permission)
- Voice recordings (optional, for voice introductions)

### 2.2 Profile Information
- Bio and about me text
- Interests and hobbies
- Education and occupation
- Relationship preferences
- Languages spoken

### 2.3 Usage Data
- App interactions and preferences
- Swipe patterns and matching preferences
- Messages sent and received
- Device information and identifiers
- Log data and analytics

### 2.4 Technical Data
- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Device type and operating system
- Time zone and location settings

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain our dating service
- Create and manage your account
- Match you with compatible users based on your preferences
- Personalize your experience and recommendations
- Communicate with you about updates, features, and promotions
- Ensure safety and prevent fraud, harassment, and abuse
- Improve our services through analytics
- Comply with legal obligations

## 4. Data Sharing

We may share your information with:
- **Other Users**: Profile information you choose to make public
- **Service Providers**: Companies that help us operate (hosting, analytics, payment processing)
- **Legal Authorities**: When required by law or to protect safety
- **Business Partners**: With your explicit consent only

We never sell your personal data to third parties.

## 5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including:
- Encryption of data in transit and at rest
- Secure authentication systems
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and monitoring

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correct**: Update inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Delete**: Request deletion of your account and data
- **Export**: Download your data in a portable format
- **Withdraw Consent**: Opt out of optional data collection
- **Restrict Processing**: Limit how we use your data
- **Object**: Object to certain types of processing

## 7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. After account deletion:
- Most data is deleted within 30 days
- Some data may be retained for legal compliance (up to 3 years)
- Anonymized data may be kept for analytics

## 8. Cookies and Tracking

We use analytics tools to understand app usage and improve our services. You can manage tracking preferences in your device settings.

## 9. Children's Privacy

GreenGo is intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we discover that a minor has created an account, we will delete it immediately.

## 10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or email. Your continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
