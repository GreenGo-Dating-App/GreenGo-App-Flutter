Blocs are exercised with mocktail-stubbed repositories (dartz Either) and expectLater(bloc.stream, emitsInOrder([...])). Widgets pump inside MaterialApp with the app's localization delegates and mockNetworkImagesFor. Datasource/query tests seed a FakeFirebaseFirestore mirroring the production seeder shapes. Backend rules/functions/performance (B1–B6) require the Firebase emulator and are the next phase.
