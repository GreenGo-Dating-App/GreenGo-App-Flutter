GreenGo · greengo-chat · v2.2.4+100  /  Migration Plan  /  2026-07-07

From a Firebase monolith to a platform built for millions.

An enterprise migration of GreenGo to Google Cloud — Kubernetes, a managed data platform, and full infrastructure-as-code — engineered so the app stays live and earning through every step.

The thesis: not a big-bang rewrite. A strangler-fig migration that keeps Firestore for what it's best at — realtime chat, presence, feeds, fan-out — and moves the relational, transactional, and analytical domains onto GKE + AlloyDB, one domain at a time, every step reversible.

Locked decisions · approved 2026-07-07

Six choices that anchor the whole design

Each is recorded as an Architecture Decision Record. Everything downstream — schemas, pipelines, cost — follows from these.

ADR-0001 · Posture

Hybrid strangler-fig

Keep Firestore for realtime; migrate relational/money/analytics domain-by-domain behind a facade.

Why App stays live and earning; risk bounded per domain; keeps GreenGo's proven realtime patterns.
ADR-0002 · Data

AlloyDB for PostgreSQL

Ledger, social graph, and discovery vectors (pgvector) on regional Postgres — not Spanner.

Why ACID + reconciliation + read-pool scale + built-in vectors at a fraction of Spanner cost. Spanner stays as an escape hatch.
ADR-0003 · Compute

GKE Autopilot + mesh

~200 functions consolidate into 14 containerized services under Anthos Service Mesh.

Why No node ops, mTLS by default, canary traffic-splitting, supports future WebSocket tiers.
ADR-0004 · Events

Pub/Sub backbone

Pub/Sub + Eventarc + Cloud Tasks replace inline Firestore-trigger sprawl.

Why Decoupled, buffered, replayable — fan-out spikes absorbed instead of dropped.
ADR-0005 · Geography

Single region first

Launch europe-west1, designed multi-region-ready; Americas region in Phase 7.

Why Faster, cheaper launch; documented MAU/latency triggers to go global.
ADR-0006 · IaC

Terraform + Argo CD

Terraform provisions infra; Argo CD reconciles Kubernetes via GitOps; Argo Rollouts does canaries.

Why Reproducible, reviewable, auditable — replaces the repo's broken Terraform and deploy scripts.

Top risks & how we hold them

Where this could go wrong — and the guardrails

high

Ledger data loss or drift during the money migration

Dual-write + Dataflow backfill + nightly reconciliation + reversible cohort cutover; no destructive delete until 100% soak, clean reconcile, and verified PITR.

high

Cost blow-up from Firestore reads and media egress

Load-test-driven budget model, CDN + cache tiers, listener audit before scaling, per-squad cost showback and budget kill-switches.

med

Migration debt causing double-migration

Phase 1 dedupes the duplicate coin*, blocked_users, and notification schemas and function folders before any domain moves.

med

Client-exposed Cloud TTS key abuse

Rotated and proxied server-side in Phase 1 with per-user rate limiting; App Check enforced; no keys ship in the client again.

med

Team unfamiliar with GKE / mesh / AlloyDB

A platform enabling team owns the golden path; Phase 2 is the low-risk learning domain; training and pairing plan in doc 12.
