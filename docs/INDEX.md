### Event ticketing & payments — [`to_actualize/event_ticketing_payments/`](to_actualize/event_ticketing_payments/)
Strategy + Brazil/US/worldwide compliance plan for selling **in‑person event tickets** with a **USD 2.99 platform fee** (organizer = merchant of record via Stripe Connect split → **taxed only on the fee**). Covers the entity-route matrix (BR parent + US payment LLC · US LLC only · autônomo validation), the margin lever, app‑store exemption, and the Firestore/Cloud‑Functions build.
- [`README.md`](to_actualize/event_ticketing_payments/README.md) — full plan + open decisions
