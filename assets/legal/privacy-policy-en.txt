1. DEFINITIONS
================================================================================

For the purposes of this Privacy Policy:

"Personal Data" means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person, including but not limited to name, identification number, location data, online identifier, or factors specific to the physical, physiological, genetic, mental, economic, cultural, or social identity of that person.

"Sensitive Personal Data" means personal data revealing racial or ethnic origin, religious beliefs, political opinions, trade union membership, health data, genetic data, biometric data, sexual orientation, or data concerning sex life.

"Processing" means any operation performed on personal data, including collection, recording, organization, structuring, storage, adaptation, alteration, retrieval, consultation, use, disclosure, dissemination, alignment, combination, restriction, erasure, or destruction.

"Data Subject" means the natural person to whom the personal data relates.

"Controller" means the natural or legal person that determines the purposes and means of processing personal data.

"Processor" means a natural or legal person that processes personal data on behalf of the Controller.

"Consent" means freely given, specific, informed, and unambiguous indication of the data subject's wishes by which they signify agreement to the processing of personal data.

"Anonymization" means processing of personal data in such a manner that the data can no longer be attributed to a specific data subject without the use of additional information.

2.5 MOBILE APPLICATION PERMISSIONS
================================================================================

Our mobile application requires certain device permissions to function properly. Below is a comprehensive list of permissions we may request and how they are used:

a) Camera Permission (android.permission.CAMERA)
   - Purpose: To allow you to take photos for your profile, capture images for identity verification, and share photos in messages with other users.
   - Data Use: Photos taken using the camera are uploaded to our servers for profile display, identity verification, or message sharing.
   - Storage: Images are stored securely on our servers in accordance with our data retention policies.
   - Third-Party Sharing: Profile photos may be visible to other users. Verification photos are processed by our identity verification service providers.
   - User Control: You can revoke camera permission at any time through your device settings. Some features requiring photos will be unavailable without this permission.

b) Location Permission (android.permission.ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION, android.permission.ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION)
   - Purpose: To show you potential matches near your location and enable location-based features.
   - Data Use: Location data is used to calculate distances and suggest nearby users.
   - Storage: Location data is stored on our servers and updated when you use the app.
   - Third-Party Sharing: Approximate location may be shown to other users (city/region level). Precise location is not shared with other users.
   - User Control: You can disable location permissions at any time. The app will function with limited features without location access.

c) Storage Permission (android.permission.READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, android.permission.WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE)
   - Purpose: To allow you to select and upload photos from your device gallery.
   - Data Use: Selected photos are uploaded to our servers for profile or messaging purposes.
   - Storage: We do not access or store any files other than those you explicitly select to upload.
   - Third-Party Sharing: Only photos you choose to share are uploaded and potentially visible to other users.
   - User Control: You can revoke storage permission at any time. Photo upload from gallery will be unavailable without this permission.

d) Internet Access (android.permission.INTERNET)
   - Purpose: Required for the app to communicate with our servers, load content, and enable messaging.
   - Data Use: All data transmitted over the internet is encrypted using TLS 1.3.
   - User Control: This permission is essential for app functionality and cannot be disabled.

e) Network State (android.permission.ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE)
   - Purpose: To check internet connectivity and optimize data usage.
   - Data Use: Used only to determine if the device is connected to the internet.
   - User Control: This permission is essential for app functionality.

f) Push Notifications (android.permission.POST_NOTIFICATIONS)
   - Purpose: To send you notifications about new messages, matches, and important updates.
   - Data Use: Notification tokens are stored to deliver push notifications.
   - Third-Party Sharing: We use Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google) to deliver notifications.
   - User Control: You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings or in-app preferences.

g) Vibration (android.permission.VIBRATE)
   - Purpose: To provide haptic feedback for notifications and interactions.
   - Data Use: No data is collected through this permission.
   - User Control: You can control vibration settings through your device settings.

h) Wake Lock (android.permission.WAKE_LOCK)
   - Purpose: To ensure notifications are delivered even when the device is in sleep mode.
   - Data Use: No data is collected through this permission.

i) Biometric Authentication (android.permission.USE_BIOMETRIC)
   - Purpose: To provide secure login using fingerprint or face recognition.
   - Data Use: Biometric data is processed locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers.
   - User Control: You can choose to use password authentication instead.

HOW TO MANAGE PERMISSIONS:

Android Users:
1. Go to Settings > Apps > GreenGo Chat > Permissions
2. Toggle individual permissions on or off
3. Some features may be limited when permissions are disabled

iOS Users:
1. Go to Settings > GreenGo Chat
2. Toggle individual permissions on or off
3. Some features may be limited when permissions are disabled

We only request permissions that are necessary for the app's core functionality or optional features you choose to use. We do not access any data beyond what is necessary for the stated purposes.
